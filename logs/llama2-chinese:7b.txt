### Who Is Sana Kuna?
Sana Kuna is a popular Japanese social media personality known for her beauty, fashion sense and lifestyle content on platforms such as YouTube, Instagram and TikTok. She has over 2 million followers across her social media platforms. She is also the founder of the fashion brands "SANA KUNABY" and "KUNAPRO", providing fans a way to support her work and connect with her more directly.
