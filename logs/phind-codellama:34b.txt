"\"Sakana-kun\" (サカナクン) is a Japanese term that can have different meanings depending on the context. In general, it refers to a young man or boy, but it may also indicate specific person who likes fishing. The character \"saka\" (魚) means fish and \"na\" (名) means fame, or someone famous in something. Therefore, \"Sakana-kun\" could be interpreted as a popular/famous young boy or man involved in fishing, but the term can also be used casually to refer to any ordinary young boy or man who likes fishing."
