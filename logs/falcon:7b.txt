Ganguro is a Japanese subculture that focuses on girls in retro, vintage clothing with a unique look and style. The name Ganguro is derived from the Ganguro style, which was popularized in the 1950s by Japanese actress Meiko Kaji. Her signature retro look influenced the subculture, which can be seen in many Ganguro outfits.
