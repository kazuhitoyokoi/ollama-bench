 Japanese: Japanese

Facts about Japanese fish stick:
Fish fry sticks are very popular in Japan, and is usually deep-fried. It is prepared with various kinds of seafood such as salmon, tuna and shrimp. The fish are marinated before they are fried, sometimes even breaded so that it gives a crunchy texture to the stick. Sometimes they are made into the form of shapes depending on what foods are in season or being popular at the time in Japan. They are sold in convenience stores, markets and even street vendors. It can come with sauces such as wasabi mayonnaise and even tartar sauce to be a complete meal.
