 "さかなクン" (sakanakun) is an informal, cute way of saying "fish" in Japanese language. It's often used by children or when you would like to speak about a fish in a more friendly and playful manner. The 'kun' is often added to names as a term of endearment in Japanese language when addressing children or males. So, Sakanakun can be thought of as "little fish friend".
