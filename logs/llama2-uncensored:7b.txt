Sayaka Kondo, known as Aikawa Sayaka in her professional career, was born on January 4, 1985, in Okazaki, Japan. She received a Bachelor of Arts degree in vocal performance from Toho Gakuen College Of Music in Tokyo. As she is fluent in both English and Japanese, she has gained popularity outside of her native country as well due to her international collaborations. She made her debut with the single "Koi ni Omatasan" in 2004 under King Record which was released exclusively in Japan. In an interview with The Japan Times, Aikawa revealed that she chose the name Sayaka Kondō from her childhood idol Kyoko Kishida's (known as Kyu Sakamoto) real name "Sayako Kishi" and combined it with Matsudaira Naosuke.
